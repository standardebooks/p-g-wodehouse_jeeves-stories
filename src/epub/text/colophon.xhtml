Jeeves Stories
was compiled from short stories published between 1915 and 1925 by
P. G. Wodehouse.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
B. Timothy Keith,
and is based on transcriptions produced between 2003 and 2019 by
Tim Lindell, Martin Pettit, Suzanne L. Shell, Charles Franks, and The Online Distributed Proofreading Team
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans available at the
Internet Archive (The Inimitable Jeeves, The Man with Two Left Feet, The Saturday Evening Post, The Strand Magazine 1916⁠–⁠17 and Carry On, Jeeves).

The cover page is adapted from
The Arrow Collars Man,
a painting completed around 1922 by
J. C. Leyendecker.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
